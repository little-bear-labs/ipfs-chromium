pure virtual

The requested path DOES NOT EXIST, conclusively, regardless of gateway.

Cannot occur without a path, as it indicates a directory (sharded or otherwise) was successfully retrieved but lacked the link necessary to descend farther down the path.

Parameters
cid- String representation of the root of the DAG
path- The path that could not be resolved.
